Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after 30-day trial?

You can continue using the billing system forever for free, but with limited features: only 3-day history visible, no export, no monthly sheets. Upgrade to Standard to unlock all features and unlimited history.

Can I pay monthly instead of yearly?

Currently we only offer annual billing (‚Çπ2000/year). This keeps costs low for dairy shop owners. Monthly plans may be available in the future.

Is my data safe?

Yes! Your data is stored securely and backed up automatically. You can download backups anytime (Standard plan). We never sell or share your data.

Can I use this offline?

Yes! MilkRecord works offline. Data is saved locally and syncs when you're back online. Perfect for areas with unstable internet.

What if I have multiple dairy branches?

Our Multi-branch add-on (coming soon) will support multiple locations with consolidated reporting. Contact us for enterprise solutions.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes! If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of paid subscription, we'll refund 100%. No questions asked.

Can I upgrade later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade from Trial to Standard anytime. Your existing data will carry over seamlessly.

Is GST invoice available?

Yes! Standard plan includes GST-ready monthly summary reports. GST filing integration is coming in Growth add-ons.
