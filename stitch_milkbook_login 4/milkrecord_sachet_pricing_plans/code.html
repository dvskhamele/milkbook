Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans.

How does the Daily Wallet work? expand_more

It's simple. You recharge your wallet with any amount (e.g., ₹100). Every day you use the app to record milk collection, ₹3 is deducted. If you don't use the app, no money is deducted.

Can I switch plans later? expand_more

Yes, you can upgrade from Free to Daily or Daily to Premium at any time. Your wallet balance will be adjusted accordingly.

Is my data safe? expand_more

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption to store your data. It is backed up daily to ensure you never lose your records.
